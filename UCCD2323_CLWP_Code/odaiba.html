About Odaiba

Odaiba is a futuristic entertainment hub built on a man-made island in Tokyo Bay. Originally constructed for defensive purposes in the 1850s, it was redeveloped in the 1990s as a high-tech leisure and shopping district with stunning views of Tokyo's skyline.

Key Attractions

  • TeamLab Borderless - immersive digital art museum
  • DiverCity Tokyo Plaza - shopping with life-size Gundam statue
  • SEGA Joypolis - indoor theme park with arcade games
  • Odaiba Seaside Park - artificial beach with Rainbow Bridge views

Travel Information

Location: Tokyo Bay, Japan

Transport: 15-20 minutes by Yurikamome line from Shimbashi Station

Best Time to Visit: Evenings for illuminated city views

Development: Redeveloped in the 1990s as a futuristic district
